Validators

ICON validators verify blockchain transactions and earn ICX based on the votes they receive. Many of them work within the ICON ecosystem, and some offer voting incentives.


sICX holders: Choose where to delegate votes for the sICX in your wallet.


bOMM holders: Influence the default delegation for sICX and the amount validators earn from the Omm staking fee.
